The magic of the Lost city

After staying in the lockdown for over a year, Nooni is now visiting her Ajja-Ajji in Somanahalli. Memories of excavating the famous stepwell and experiencing village life for the first time in The Magic of the Lost Temple are still afresh in Nooni’s mind.

Excited to finally step out of the confines of her home, little does Nooni know she will make yet another discovery, only this time it’s a missing puzzle in her family’s history.

This city, known as Eldoria, was said to have vanished from the face of the earth centuries ago, leaving behind only whispers of its existence. Many had sought its hidden treasures and secrets, but none had returned.
